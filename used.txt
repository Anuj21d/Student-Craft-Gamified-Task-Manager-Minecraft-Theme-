# Design System Theme

 

## Overview

This document outlines the core visual theme for our design system, reflecting a modern and purposeful aesthetic.

 

## Color Palette

Our color palette is meticulously crafted to ensure accessibility, brand recognition, and a harmonious user experience.

The primary mode for this theme is `dark`.

 

### Brand Colors

- **Primary Color:** `#5DBA3E` - A vibrant green, symbolizing growth and vitality, used for key interactive elements and calls to action.

- **Secondary Color:** `#795548` - A warm brown, providing a grounded contrast and supporting secondary UI elements.

- **Tertiary Color:** `#828282` - A neutral grey, acting as an accent for highlights and decorative details.

- **Neutral Color:** `#1E1E1E` - A deep, dark tone forming the base for backgrounds and surfaces.

 

## Typography

Our typography system is designed for readability and hierarchy, ensuring a clear and engaging content presentation.

 

### Font Families

All textual elements, including headlines, body text, and labels, consistently use the `spaceGrotesk` font, providing a cohesive and modern typographic voice throughout the interface.

 

## Shape and Form

The geometric properties of our UI elements contribute significantly to the overall feel and recognition of the brand.

 

### Roundedness

UI elements feature sharp, angular corners (`0`). This choice provides a modern, precise, and confident aesthetic, creating a distinct visual identity that emphasizes clarity and structure.

 

## Spacing

Our spacing system is built on a consistent scale, promoting visual balance and ease of comprehension across all layouts.

 

### Density

The spacing across our UI is `normal` (`2`), providing a balanced amount of whitespace that is comfortable for reading and interaction without feeling overly dense or sparse. This offers a good balance between information density and visual breathing room.
